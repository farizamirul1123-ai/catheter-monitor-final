Project Mission & Goals

This system is your final project, aimed at enhancing patient safety and monitoring through IoT and AI Edge Impulse technology. We integrate two main modules:

  • Camera Module (ESP32-CAM): Detection of **Normal Urine** vs. **Pyuria Urine** (Turbidity).
  • Weight Module (Arduino/ESP32): Measurement of urine weight to trigger alerts at thresholds of 500g, 1000g, and critical 1500g.

Critical Goals:

Ensure real-time notifications are sent to Telegram when Pyuria is detected or the critical weight limit of 1.5kg is reached, preventing infection risk and catheter overflow.
